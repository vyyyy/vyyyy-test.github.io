Dropping out

Hi, my name is Vyoma. If like me you’re a new programmer, then, I hope you find my curated list of learning resources useful. I began my self-directed study into programming and machine learning 1.5 < x > 1.0 years ago and kept a record of what I’ve used since day one.

I dropped out of a research degree from Sydney Medical School at The University of Sydney at the end of 2014. I lasted a couple weeks. I wanted to become a scientist but what I found was this stuck in the past method of trying to solve problems with last century thinking and tools. I realised that this outdated approach to research was becoming irrelevant now and would be entirely vestigial by the time I graduated with some phd in 5 years.

At the same time, I started reading about machine learning and its application in science and medicine but I had no idea how this technology worked and I was code illiterate. I would not have been able to read or write a single line of code. So, I went back to square one learning to program and now I’m looking to the future. A future that I want to help create. Yeah, I’m completely sold on Demis Hassabis’ vision of building AI scientists.

I’m still learning so if you have suggestions for me (books/papers/courses), let me know! Say hi - DM/email.
